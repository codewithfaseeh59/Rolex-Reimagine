The Art of Making

The Rolex
Process

01

In-House Movements

Every Rolex movement is designed, developed, and manufactured entirely in-house — a complete integration that ensures uncompromising quality at every stage.

02

Oystersteel Mastery

Rolex uses its own exclusive alloy — Oystersteel — from the 904L steel family. Particularly resistant to corrosion, it achieves a rare sheen when polished.

03

Superlative Chronometer

Every Rolex is certified as a Superlative Chronometer — accurate to -2/+2 seconds per day. A standard that goes far beyond industry requirements.

04

Perpetual Rotor

The invention of the self-winding mechanism with a Perpetual rotor in 1931 transformed watchmaking. It winds the mainspring continuously through the motion of the wrist.
